About CareerCompass

CareerCompass is a comprehensive resume analysis and HR tool designed to help job seekers and HR professionals alike.

Use Case

  • Job Seekers: Upload your resume to get detailed analysis of your skills, experience, and education. Receive personalized course recommendations and job opportunities tailored to your profile.
  • HR Professionals: Manage candidate profiles, filter applicants based on job requirements, and streamline your hiring process with our intuitive dashboard.
  • Our mission is to empower individuals to unlock their career potential and help organizations find the best talent efficiently.
